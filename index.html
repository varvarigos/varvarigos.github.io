About Me

I am a Ph.D. student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at Yale University, advised by Prof. Leandros Tassiulas. My work sits at the intersection of machine learning, deep learning, and networked systems, with a focus on building models and tools that are rigorous, practical, and useful for real-world infrastructure.

Before Yale, I completed my M.Eng. in Electronic and Information Engineering at Imperial College London. My broader interests include graph machine learning, large language models for technical domains, optimization, computer vision, and trustworthy ML systems.
